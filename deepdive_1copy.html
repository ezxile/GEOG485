Cities With the Most Park Space

This is a map of the top ten cities in the United States. Park Score ranks cities by determining the ratio between natural park space and developed space within the city limits. Park Score does this for cities around the world; yet this is a map of the highest ranked cities in the U.S. Interestingly, Albuquerque is ranked 11th in the nation. Unfortunately it was not placed in this map.
