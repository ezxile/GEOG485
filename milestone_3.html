Question 1

What area do you want to depict in your map? Why?

  • I want to depict the available park space in the United States. I am interested in showing the access that people have to natural public space.

Question 2

What is the center point (latitude and longitude) of your area of interest?

  • The center point of the map is (40.683407,-102.0983269) which is an ambiguous point to center the map with the best center in the frame

Question 3

What style of map (roads, satellite, hybrid, terrain) is an appropriate for your map? Why?

  • Roads style for the map is most appropriate because the color blocking of land use type makes reading locations for parks more fluid.

Question 4

What is the scale of your map (local, regional, continental, global)? How will this translate into your selection of an appropriate default zoom level for your map?

  • This map is on a regional scale. This scale is ideal because it shows most of the United States in a way that allows the user to scroll around the map to get an understanding of green space and to zoom into desired locations to understand the data at a local scale.
